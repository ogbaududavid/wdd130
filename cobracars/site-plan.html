Cobra-cars

Cobra cars contains information’s about two major car manufacturers and why they are preferred in Nigeria. Also in exploring the reasons why these manufacturers and the specific models are preferred, in a few “did you know” fun fact, you’ll get to uncover interesting information’s about functionalities you probably never knew existed in these models and how they affect performance. TOYOTA

General Functionality If you live in Nigeria, then you must own a Toyota car, it is almost as through Toyota’s (Corolla’s more precisely) were built specifically with Nigeria in mind. Toyota cars have a high ground clearance, making it easy for them to navigate bad and bumpy roads which are a common sight in Nigeria. They are usually very easy to maintain and their parts cheap and readily available. In a city like Lagos, look around and you are sure to see a Toyota vehicle drive by, it is for a reason. Due to the high rate at which people use these manufacturer in Nigeria and outside Nigeria, it is very easy to access their used spare parts at relatively cheaper prices compared to the new ones. Over the years these models have won the hearts of Nigerians as a dependent road companion. Price Their prices range from a few millions to tens of millions, and are also available in used car lots at affordable prices. If you are interested in buying a Toyota car in Nigeria you can visit www.toyotanigeria.com the official website. Also, Toyotas have a generally high resale value in the Nigerian automobile market. People generally have high trust level for corolla cars as they know that damages can be easily fixed, even by a road side mechanic. Maintenance The cost of maintaining Corolla are relatively low compared to other vehicles, basically, what you need to do is ensure you have you regular oil change as at when due. There are many technicians ready to provide services in the case where you need to carry out a repair. Watching out for signs of wear and strange sounds also save you a great maintenance cost.

MERCEDES BENZ

Are you a fan of comfort and style? Then the Mercedes Benz is for you. The C-Class is a mid-range exotic sedan that offers comfort and style at a reasonable price range and there are other models that stand out even to highly exotic for the super reach and influential people of the society. “Benz” as they are fondly called in Nigeria are built for performance and an effortless show of class and style. These cars do well in Nigerian climate. The paint job, although requiring a little extra maintenance are able to last long without deteriorating. It is important to note that due to the high amount of electrical parts and sensors Mercedes Benz cars are not suitable to drive in water logged areas which are common in Nigeria, so, you might want to keep your “Benz” off the road when it rains and there’s possibility of flood water rising above 12 inches. Price They are a very expensive ranging in millions of Naira, but considering the value they offer and compared to their counterparts, are worth the price. They are also available in used conditions, which are relatively cheaper compared to the new ones, but be careful when buying a used “Benz” from used lot,s as you might be buying an already damaged car with electric issues that can be difficult to fix. Maintenance For effective performance use the recommended oil type and only service this vehicles at their approved service centers. Do not use roadside or quack mechanics for repairs as they might in many cases cause severe damages to parts due to the complexity of the build and parts
