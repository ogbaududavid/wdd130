TOYOTA

Price

Their prices range from a few millions to tens of millions, and are also available in used car lots at affordable prices. If you are interested in buying a Toyota car in Nigeria you can visit www.toyotanigeria.com the official website. Also, Toyotas have a generally high resale value in the Nigerian automobile market. People generally have high trust level for corolla cars as they know that damages can be easily fixed, even by a road side mechanic.

Maintenance

The cost of maintaining Toyotas are relatively low compared to other vehicles, basically, what you need to do is ensure you have you regular oil change as at when due. There are many technicians ready to provide services in the case where you need to carry out a repair. Watching out for signs of wear and strange sounds also save you a great maintenance cost.
