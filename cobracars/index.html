Toyota

General Functionality If you live in Nigeria, then you shold own a Toyota car, it is almost as though Toyotas (Corolla’s more precisely) were built specifically with Nigeria in mind. Toyota cars have a high ground clearance, making it easy for them to navigate bad and bumpy roads which are a common sight in Nigeria. They are usually very easy to maintain and their parts cheap and readily available. In a city like Lagos, look around and you are sure to see a Toyota vehicle drive by, it is for a reason. Due to the high rate at which people use these manufacturer in Nigeria and outside Nigeria, it is very easy to access their used spare parts at relatively cheaper prices compared to the new ones. Over the years these models have won the hearts of Nigerians as a dependent road companion.

Mercedex-Benz

Are you a fan of comfort and style? Then the Mercedes Benz is for you. The C-Class is a mid-range exotic sedan that offers comfort and style at a reasonable price range and there are other models that stand out even to highly exotic for the super reach and influential people of the society. “Benz” as they are fondly called in Nigeria are built for performance and an effortless show of class and style. These cars do well in Nigerian climate. The paint job, although requiring a little extra maintenance are able to last long without deteriorating. It is important to note that due to the high amount of electrical parts and sensors Mercedes Benz cars are not suitable to drive in water logged areas which are common in Nigeria, so, you might want to keep your “Benz” off the road when it rains and there’s possibility of flood water rising above 12 inches.
