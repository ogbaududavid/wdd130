MERCEDEZ-BENZ

Price

They are a generally very pricey, ranging from tens of millions of Naira to over hundreds of millions, but, considering the value they offer and compared to their counterparts, are worth the price. They are also available in used conditions, which are relatively cheaper compared to the new ones. Be careful when buying a used “Benz” from used lots where they are sold as you might be buying an already damaged car with electrical issues that can be difficult to fix. If you must buy a used Benz, visit an authorized dealership.

Maintenance

For effective performance, use the recommended oil type and only service this vehicles at their approved service centers. Do not use roadside or quack mechanics for repairs as they might in many cases cause severe damages to parts. Things can get really complex
